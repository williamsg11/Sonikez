About Our Company

We only work and do business with reputable partners.

Sonikez, is an international and trading company, based in Nottingham United Kingdom, doing business in a wide range of goods but mainly focused on Fast Moving Consumer Goods FMCG like Personal care products, office stationery, confectionery, ect with worldwide sales, a diverse and dynamic group.

We are a family run company that has been operating for many years now, which enabled us to gain vast experience in the market. We source and trade goods in niche markets around the world. We only work and do business with reputable partners, so we can deliver better results to our customers. Today, Sonikez has continued to evolve and grow with absolute integrity, transparency and responsibility towards our partners and clients.
